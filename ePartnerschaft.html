partnership Eikwe, Ghana- SW

For over a decade, the Walther-Rathenau Schools have had partner schools in Eikwe, Ghana.
They have a good exchange program with initiatives like the 11th-grade Solar Project, 12th-grade Art Project, and the student group "Schüler fair-ändern die Welt".
Moreover the student group “Schüler fair ändern die Welt” made a video about the partnership between the Ghanaian schools and the "Rathenau".
