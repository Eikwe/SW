Installation of a solar panel system for our partner school

Solar project of the 11th grade students.

In collaboration with our partner school, the St. Luke Vocational School in Ghana, we intend to install a solar panel system to ensure the school's self-sufficient power supply.
This initiative will also offer the vocational students a practical opportunity to learn about the innovative technology of solar energy.
