The utopia behind our project

Our project "Our energy gives you energy" is inspired by the African philosophy of Ubuntu. This philosophy states that our own well-being is inseparably linked to the well-being of our community. Everyday situations such as eating a banana illustrate this connection: from planting to harvesting, numerous people are involved. What is crucial about Ubuntu is that as human beings, we should support each other to lead a fulfilling and happy life collectively.

Our project puts these ideas into practice by installing a solar panel system in collaboration with our partner school. Overall, our project represents a social and ecological utopia.
