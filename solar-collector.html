big success:

The solar system was successfully set up at our partnering institution, St. Luke's School in Eikwe, Ghana. Thanks to the generous donations received, the solar installation could be significantly expanded beyond its initial scope.

<
>

The students of the Electrical Department were provided with practical and theoretical insights into solar system design and operation by the nonprofit organization naBiG. This knowledge enables them to contribute to the installation of more solar systems in Eikwe, fostering the community's transition to renewable energy. It's worth highlighting the enthusiasm of these young Ghanaians to learn and make the most of the opportunities offered by naBiG to grasp this new technology.

Furthermore, Bishop Attakruh honored us with his presence, highlighting the appreciation from the Ghanaian community. Overall, communication between our school and the partner institution has been excellent.
