On this page, you will find information about the projects of the Walther-Rathenau schools.


installation of the solar panel

The solar panel system has been installed at our partner school, St. Luke's School in Eikwe, Ghana, thanks to the generous donations received. Due to these contributions, we were able to make the solar installation significantly larger than originally planned. A significant portion of the funds came from the cable collection campaign, and we want to express our gratitude to everyone who donated faulty cables in May.
